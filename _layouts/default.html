Joyce Ovalle

The University of Texas at Austin

  • CV

I am a PhD Candidate at UT Austin’s LBJ School of Public Affairs. My research interests are on the role of civil society organizations, particularly environmental nonprofits and their funders, in shaping public policies. My professional journey includes roles at the Urban Institute's Center on Nonprofits and Philanthropy, research positions at Texas A&M University, and contributions to the public sector through service with the U.S. Department of Energy. I am a proud first-generation alumnus of Texas Public Schools.
